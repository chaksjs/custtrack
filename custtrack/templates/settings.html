📋 How to get a Gmail App Password (step-by-step)
  1. Go to myaccount.google.com/security
  2. Make sure 2-Step Verification is turned ON (required)
  3. Go to myaccount.google.com/apppasswords
  4. Under "App name", type CustTrack and click Create
  5. Google shows a 16-character password (e.g. abcd efgh ijkl mnop)
  6. Copy it and paste it in the field below (spaces are OK)
  7. Click 💾 Save & Test — you should see a ✅ success message
⚠️ This password is different from your Gmail login password. Never share it.
